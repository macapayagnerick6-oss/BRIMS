Help & Contact

For login issues, password reset, or account help, please get in touch with your barangay office.

Account registration. To create a BRIMS account, please go to your barangay office in person. Barangay staff will assist you with registration and set up your access.

Visit or call your barangay hall during office hours, or ask for the staff in charge of BRIMS (Barangay Resident Information Management System).

They can help you with access credentials and password resets.
